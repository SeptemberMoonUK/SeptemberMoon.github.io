Our story

September Moon began with a love for beautiful childhood pieces — the kind that feel timeless, soft, and full of meaning.

What started as a small passion quickly grew into a space for carefully curated and handmade items, chosen for their quality, charm, and attention to detail.

Every piece you see here is selected with care — whether it’s vintage, contemporary, or handmade — with the aim of creating something special for families and little ones.

September Moon is about slowing down, choosing thoughtfully, and celebrating the beauty in small things.
